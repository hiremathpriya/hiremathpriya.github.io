The Tonglen meditation. Tonglen meditation "It's about the generosity of an open heart and mind, we imagine ourself sitting in front of ourself, and Looking at our “ordinary self,” with its suffering—whatever may be troubles and sadnness inside. we Breathe this in as if it is a dark cloud of gunk, and let it disperse and transform. and Breathe it out as rays of light.” We do this for our ownself and for others who are suferring. I found this quite useful in terms of seeing my ownself and my sufferings when I closed my eyes and had to think about what are my troubles and sufferings at this stage then only one thing started rolling in my head was a bubble...

A javascript bubble haunting me, Code.. code everywhere and I am lost in that bubble :) I could see my suffering here. But with the Tonglen meditation practice I feel it will make it easy to accept our suferings , and transform those suffering with the ray of light and understand ourselves better.

Thinking about someone else's suferings Yes there are quite a few people about whom I feel and know their sufferings and wish I could relive their sufferings, With meditation or without I can understand their pain and what they are going through, I hope that ray of light comes into their life soon as well.

Comparing SCARF model with the phase 0 experience so far, Phase 0 experience is been very interesting, challenging, a big learning curve in my life so far. SCARF model fits quite nicely with the phase 0 experience in terms of

Status

“When you gain increasing mastery over something that matters to you, you activate a status reward, at least when compared against your former self.” It is been like learning something for the first time and unable to understand a thing, but over the peroid of time you are getting really good at it. It's been expaclty like this with all the concepts in this phase 0 challenges. when you look back at where you started and how much understanding you have gained by this time it's absoultly staus reward. for e.g When I started learning about HTML and now when I write a blog..it's a reward.

Certinity

With phase 0 lot of the times you try to learn somethning but still can't get it and that's the time you start to get uncertain about your progress and it eats you inside. I think In phase 0 Certinity and uncertainity plays quite a big role. “If you do not know whether your job is secure, uncertainty will probably occupy most of your mind and you may not be able to do much else at work.

Autonomy

It’s not the stress itself that gets to you; it’s the feeling of helplessness in the face of that stress. It is so true in phase 0 When you are so stuck at some of the challenges and how hard you try to understand you still don't get it. Then it's that feeling of helplesness which actucally gets you, more than the stress.

Relatedness

This is the phase where you meet a lot of stangers and start your journey. “When you get to my age, you’ll measure your success in life by how many of the people you want to have love you actually do love you. That’s the ultimate test of how you’ve lived your life.” By default, the brain labels someone a foe “unless proven otherwise. For example, strangers are usually labeled as foe (or at least, labeled as “approach at your own risk”). Fortunately, in many situations, it is not hard to switch people from foes to friends. For example, all it usually takes is a handshake and a pleasant conversation.

Fairness

Being fair to your ownself, others and your learning is the key.
