I have to start by saying emotional intelligence starts by "Just being in the present moment" and experience the goodness it brings to all of us. There are quite a few things I have learned from this chapter they are as follows.Being optimistic that emotional intelligence is one of the best predictors of success at work and fulfillment in life “The ability to monitor one’s own and others’ feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one’s thinking and actions. Emotional competencies are something you can deliberately acquire with practice.

  1. Self-awareness: Knowledge of one’s internal states, preferences, resources, and intuitions
  2. Self-regulation: Management of one’s internal states, impulses, and resources
  3. Motivation: Emotional tendencies that guide or facilitate reaching goals
  4. Empathy: Awareness of others’ feelings, needs, and concerns 

    5. Emotional intelligence enables three important skill sets: stellar work performance, outstanding leadership, and the ability to create the conditions for happiness.

    I have been practising Mindfullness for quite some time now. As being in present moment works for your happiness as well as for your general well being.
